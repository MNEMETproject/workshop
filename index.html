September 29th, 2025

Department of Economics, Business, Mathematics and Statistics “B. de Finetti"

University of Trieste


The MNEMET (Modelling Non-standard data and Extremes in Multivariate Environmental Time series) final workshop aims to bring together young researchers and subject experts for exchanging the latest scientific advancements and ideas for future research in methodological and applied areas, including multivariate statistical modelling, spatial-temporal data analysis, time series modeling, and extreme value theory in environmental problems.

The MNEMET Research Team welcomes all those interested in participating in the workshop. The speakers and the talks are:

  • Giada Adelfio, Università degli Studi di Palermo
    Improving Point Process Modelling: Accounting for Local and Inhomogeneous Mark-Weighted Second-Order Statistics
  • Roberto Casarin, Università Ca’ Foscari di Venezia
    A Spatiotemporal Gamma Shot Noise Cox Process
  • Gioia Di Credico, Università degli Studi di Trieste
    Bayesian Markov-Switching Approaches to Heatwave Analysis
  • Fabrizio Durante, Università del Salento
    Semi-Supervised Clustering of Geo-Referenced Time Series with Copulas
  • Vincenzo Gioia, Università degli Studi di Trieste
    Multivariate modeling of urban air pollutants
  • Luca Aiello, Università degli Studi di Bergamo
    Bayesian nonparametric clustering for spatio-temporal data, with an application to air pollution
  • Selene Perazzini, Libera Università di Bolzano-Bozen
    A copula-based cross-dependence coefficient for temporal data
  • Ilaria Prosdocimi, Università Ca’ Foscari di Venezia
    Non-stationarities in extreme hourly precipitation over the Piave Basin, Northern Italy
  • Francesco Ravazzolo, Libera Università di Bolzano-Bozen and BI Norwegian Business School
    Forecasting Oil Consumption: The Statistical Review of World Energy meets Machine Learning
  • Andrea Viselli, Università degli Studi di Milano
    Modeling European Electricity Market Integration during turbulent times

The one-day workshop will take place in the main campus of the University of Trieste at the Department of Economics, Business, Mathematics and Statistics “B. de Finetti” (deams.units.it).

Opening and registration: 9:00 – 9:30
Workshop end: around 4:30 PM

A tentative schedule for the day will be published soon.

Participation to the event is free and it includes a light lunch and coffee breaks.
