Research Units

MNEMET aims to bring together researchers from three Italian universities.

University of Trieste

Roberta Pappadà

Associate professor of Statistics and Principal Investigator of MNEMET. Her research interests include Copula-based modelling, Computational Statistics, Classification and clustering, Time series clustering, Machine learning algorithms, Discrimination detection, and Ensemble methods in unsupervised problems. She has published in Statistics and Computing, Spatial Statistics, Fuzzy Sets and Systems.

Gaetano Carmeci

Associate professor of Econometrics. Research interests: Bayesian and spatial econometrics, panel cointegration, forecasting of electricity prices. Published in the Journal of Macroeconomics, Journal of Risk and Insurance, Journal of Policy Modeling, and Journal of Public Economic Theory.

Giovanni Millo

Associate professor of Economic Statistics, formerly at Assicurazioni Generali. His interests are Panel Data Econometrics, Spatial Econometrics, Computational Econometrics, and Insurance Economics. Published in Journal of Applied Econometrics, Journal of Statistical Software, Journal of Spatial Econometrics, and Journal of Risk and Insurance.

Vincenzo Gioia

Post-doctoral researcher in the Department of Economics, Business, Mathematics, and Statistics. Ph.D. in Managerial and Actuarial Sciences. Research areas: semi-parametric regression, statistical computing, open-source software, with applications in engineering, environmental statistics, and life sciences.

University of Milan

Luca Rossini

Associate professor of Statistics and Senior Researcher at Fondazione Eni Enrico Mattei. Co-PI and head of the local unit. His research spans Bayesian inference, time series analysis, energy economics, and nonlinear models. Published in Journal of Business and Economic Statistics, Journal of Econometrics, International Journal of Forecasting, and Annals of Applied Statistics.

Andrea Bastianin

Associate professor of Economic Policy and Program Director at Fondazione Eni Enrico Mattei. Research interests: Time Series Econometrics, Environmental and Energy Economics, Forecasting, and Critical raw materials. Publications in Energy Economics, Macroeconomic Dynamics, Energy Policy, and Economic Modelling.

Andrea Viselli

Post-doctoral researcher in Econometrics. Focuses on time series models, nowcasting, and forecasting evaluation. Currently completing a Ph.D. at the University of Milan and Pavia under Prof. Fabrizio Iacone and Prof. Luca Rossini.

Free University of Bozen-Bolzano

F. Marta L. Di Lascio

Associate professor of Statistics and head of the local unit. Research interests: stochastic models with copulas, clustering techniques for complex data, and methods for high-dimensional spatial, time series, and panel data. Published in Biometrics, Computational Statistics and Data Analysis, Spatial Statistics, Journal of Classification, and Journal of Banking and Finance.

Selene Perazzini

Researcher in statistics focused on spatio-temporal modelling for environmental risk. Ph.D. from IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca on georeferenced data, natural risks, and insurance. Currently research assistant at Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, working on multivariate environmental time series with complex dependencies.
